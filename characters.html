Character Descriptions

Carol Peletier (The Walking Dead)

Carol transforms from a submissive housewife to a formidable survivor. Initially timid, she evolves into a strong leader who protects others and defies traditional gender roles in a post-apocalyptic world.

Katniss Everdeen (The Hunger Games)

A tough, resourceful hunter who volunteers for the Hunger Games to save her sister. She grows into a symbol of rebellion, demonstrating resilience and leadership.
